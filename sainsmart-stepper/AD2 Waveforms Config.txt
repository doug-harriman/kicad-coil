Power

|Signal| AD2 | Stepper Driver |
| ---  | --- | -------------- | 
|  5V  |  V+ | PUL+, DIR+, ENA+ |
| Step |  W1 | PUL- |
| Enable| W2 | ENA- |

Signals are active low.

Config
- Activate master power
- V+ to 5V
- W1 Square wave, 2.5V amplitude, 2.5V offset
- W2 DC, 0V to activate, 5V to disable
